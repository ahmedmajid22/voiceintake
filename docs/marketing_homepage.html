How it works
From voice to record
in three steps.
No hardware. No training. No changes to your existing systems.
STEP 01
🎙️
Patient speaks
On any phone or tablet, the patient speaks naturally for 30 seconds — their name, date of birth, insurance number, address, and symptoms. In any of our supported languages.
STEP 02
⚡
AI extracts everything
Whisper AI transcribes the audio. Llama 3.3 extracts every field with high accuracy. The patient reviews the pre-filled form and corrects anything before submitting.
STEP 03
📊
Staff sees it instantly
The completed intake record appears in the staff dashboard the moment the patient submits. Searchable, filterable, and organized by clinic location.
Features
Everything a clinic needs.
Nothing it doesn't.
🔒
GDPR & HIPAA Ready
Audio is processed in memory and never stored. Consent screen required before every recording. Only extracted text is saved.
🌍
4 Languages
English, Arabic, German, and French — natively. Patients select their language before recording. No extra setup needed.
🏥
Multi-Location
Each clinic location gets its own ID and isolated dashboard. One account, multiple locations, no data mixing.

Zero-Code Install

Patient Edits Before Submit
Every field is editable before submission. Patients fix errors themselves — no back-and-forth with reception staff.

Any Device
